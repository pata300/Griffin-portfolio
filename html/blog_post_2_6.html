1917

More than your typical war flick.

This “one-shot” epic World War I movie started off my year (and decade) in such a profound way. I’d like to preference that I’m not overly excited for movies based on wars. If I’m going to spend a few hours watching extreme violence I’ll usually sit down to a nice horror or action flick. That being said, 1917 was a completely mesmerizing adventure (check the definition, it fits). The one-shot style matched with the aesthetic of the masterful cinematography delivers pure movie magic from start to finish.
