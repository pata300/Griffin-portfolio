Introducing "FeastQuest"

Can't decide where to eat? Let's go on a FeastQuest!

We all love food and friends, right? But what happens every time we try to mix the two? INDECISIVENESS! I’ve lost count of the times I’ve gone back and forth with my significant other about agreeing on a place to chow down. With my app that struggle will soon be a thing of the past. This app can be used by anyone, regardless if their taste is simple or complex, by groups of any size, people with dietary restrictions or all out gluttons. Whether you’re looking for a quick bite or trying to go all out for an evening of drinks at a five-star restaurant, FeastQuest will serve you well.

User will be asked simple questions towards what kind of experience they’re seeking:
  • How many guest are looking to dine?
  • Would you like to dine-in or take-out?
  • What type of food (Mexican, American, Italian, etc.) is your party interested in at the moment?

Once the users have complete the form, FeastQuest will search for options within a defined perimeter of the user’s location. This will be done through Google’s “Places” API. Then the options gathered will be given to the user to pick from. All selections will be pooled into a database to help users make more precise decision in the future. FeastQuest will even give a “Select Random” button for extra indecisive users to relinquish all decision making and leave their stomach in the hands of fate.
